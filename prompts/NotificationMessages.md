### **Objective: Smart Notification System & Alarm Experience**

1. **Personalized Notification Messages**
   Replace generic alerts like *“Your task is due”* with personalized and contextual messages.
   Example:
   *“Hi, {Name}, your task ‘Assessment Tasks’ is due soon.”*
   This creates a more engaging and user-specific experience.

2. **Context-Aware Reminders**
   Notifications should include:

   * The **user’s name**
   * The **specific task title**
   * The **deadline status** (e.g., upcoming, due soon, overdue)

   This ensures users clearly understand what action is needed without confusion.

3. **Pre-Deadline Reminder Intervals**
   Implement progressive reminders before the deadline:

   * First reminder: **10 minutes before due time**
   * Follow-up reminder: **5 minutes after the first reminder (i.e., 5 minutes before deadline)**

   This repeated prompting helps keep users consistently aware and reduces missed deadlines.

4. **Full-Screen Alarm at Deadline**
   When the exact deadline is reached:

   * Trigger a **full-screen alarm notification**
   * Include clear action buttons:

     * **Dismiss**
     * **Snooze (5 minutes)**
   * Ensure the alarm is noticeable (sound + visual) to immediately capture user attention.

5. **Improved User Experience (UI/UX)**
   These enhancements aim to:

   * Increase **user engagement and responsiveness**
   * Reduce **missed tasks**
   * Provide a **clear, intuitive, and proactive reminder system**
   * Deliver a more **human-centered and interactive interface**
